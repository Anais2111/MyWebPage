There is so much power in our words that they must
be carefully selected, because the language
has incredible power to unleash wars,
to curse the glorify, to give oaths to give and take lives,
to hurt the heart and make a person cynical or cordial.
Be careful in your explanations
and remember that taming the tongue
is difficult but it is possible.
Mouths, uttering both blessings
and curses doomed to perdition.

Dianna Charisse
